Lab1. 員工和顧客在各城市各有多少人

計算customerid和employeeid有多少人在和表customers的city連結，最後再依照城市群組化
select count(o.customerid) as customertotal,count(o.employeeid) as employeetotal,c.city from orders o
join customers c on (c.customerid = o.customerid)
group by c.city
order by customertotal;

Lab2. 在那個城市的產品 賣出金額大小

select p.productname,sum(od.unitprice*od.quantity*(1-od.discount)) as money,s.city from `order details` od
join products p on (p.productid = od.productid)
join suppliers s on (s.supplierid = p.supplierid)
group by s.city
order by money desc;
